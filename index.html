Creative Direction (2024)

illRise2Shine Corporate Identity and Fashion Concepts

https://r2shine.de
← →

Rise 2 Shine reimagines how we approach apparel by challenging the need for new creations and focusing on unlocking the potential in existing materials.
This method supports sustainability and pushes creativity through reuse and transformation. Similar to architect Arno Brandlhuber, who transforms existing structures into something new and impactful, Rise 2 Shine applies this concept to fashion.
Seeking to innovate by repurposing what already exists, to create unique results. In a crowded market, Rise 2 Shine advocates for a fresh perspective that values and transforms existing resources. By remixing and reimagining current materials, the products are unique, sustainable and eye-opening. The registered trademark ® embodies this approach, echoing the simplicity and elegance seen in Apple's 2010s marketing.
Rise 2 Shine® represents a new narrative for fashion, where fun and value come from rethinking and transforming the familiar.
